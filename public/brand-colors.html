Usage Guidelines

Dark Sea Green - Primary

Use for primary actions, links, success states, and key brand moments. This is our primary brand color representing growth and success.

Golden Poppy - Accent

Use for highlights, awards, medals, and special achievements. Creates energy and draws attention to important elements.

Shadow Gray - Text & UI

Primary text color for light backgrounds. Use for headings, body copy, and UI elements requiring strong contrast.

Super Silver - Backgrounds

Light theme background color. Creates clean, modern layouts with excellent readability.

Natural Black - Backgrounds

Dark theme background color. Provides premium feel and reduces eye strain in low-light environments.

Pure White - Text & Cards

Text on dark backgrounds, card backgrounds, and clean surfaces. Ensures maximum readability and clarity.
